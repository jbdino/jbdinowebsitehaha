Felipe Agoncillo

Full Name: Felipe Agoncillo

Birth: May 26, 1859, Taal, Batangas, Philippines

Death: September 29, 1941

Profession: Lawyer, Diplomat, Revolutionary

Notable Contribution: Represented the Philippines in negotiations with foreign powers, particularly during the Treaty of Paris (1898).

Legacy: Recognized as the first Filipino diplomat and a key figure in the struggle for Philippine independence.
